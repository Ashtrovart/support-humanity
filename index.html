Our Plan of Donation

Before we jump on the field, we must have a plan. This time we have some strategies through which we can accomplish the proper distribution of the money that you have donated to us for the people who are really needy. In the below part, we have explained about them.

Food Ensurity

The very first thing they need now is food and water. There is not enough food right now. We need to ensure the food and water facility to them as early as possible to let them survived. This must be the first priority before everything.

Shelter for Homeless

Thousands of people have been homeless on this disaster. They have lost their home, money, and everything they had. Through this programme, we would like to build a new house for every family who has truly lost their last hope in this situation.

Treatment Facilities

We focused our health interventions particularly on the provision of hospital-based clinical care due to the fact that a vast majority of people in Bangladesh face barriers to accessing quality hospital-based care, resulting in thousands of deaths every year from preventable causes.
